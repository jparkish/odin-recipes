Lemon Pie

Description

The World's Best Lemon Pie, you'll never need another recipe again!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 1/4 cup Cornstarch
  • 3 tbsp All Purpose Flour
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 2 cups Water
  • 3 large Egg Yolks, beaten, room temperature
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • 1/4 cup Lemon Juice
  • 1 tsp grated Lemon Zest
  • 1 Pastry Shell (9 inches), baked
  • 3 large Egg Whites, room temperature

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine dry ingredients. Gradually stir in water and cook and stir over medium heat until thickened
  2. Gradually stir 1 cup of hot mixture into egg yolks.
  3. Stir in the butter,lemon juice and zest until smooth.
  4. In a bowl, beat egg whites and salt until stiff peaks form.
  5. Spread over pie. Bake at 350 degree until meringue is golden.
